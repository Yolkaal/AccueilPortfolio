Fate Of The Click V1

Context and project's presentation

This project was made for the Code Game Jam 2026. With my team composed of 4 friends in first year of IT, we had 30 hours to create a game from scratch on the theme "do clicks" or "click party". We decided to make a game about a folder named Foldy. The goal is to survive to waves of mouse pointers which want to click on you and discover your files.
