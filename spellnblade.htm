Spell 'n Blade

Context and project presentation

Spell 'n Blade is my main personal project. It takes place in a medieval fantasy universe mixing hero-shooter gameplay with RPG mechanics.

The goal of the project is to design a rich world with unique heroes, magical abilities, and strategic combat systems where players combine spells and weapons in battle.

The project is currently at the design and concept stage.

Game concept

The gameplay focuses on combining magic and melee combat. Each character has unique spells, abilities, and fighting styles.

Players will be able to:
• Build a hero with unique powers learnt from characters met in the game
• Combine weapons and magical abilities
• Fight in team-based battles
• Explore a fantasy universe with its own lore

World and universe

The universe of Spell 'n Blade is inspired by classic medieval fantasy. It includes multiple factions, magical creatures, and ancient powers.

The goal is to build a coherent lore with different kingdoms, mages, warriors, and mythical beings shaping the world.

Project goals

• Create a unique hero-shooter fantasy concept
• Design original characters and abilities
• Build a deep and immersive universe
• Develop gameplay mixing strategy, magic, and combat with EQUILIBRATED multiplayer competitions (It means a lot to me).
